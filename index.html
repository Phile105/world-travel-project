Why Money Heist?

The thrill of the perfect crime

Money Heist (La Casa de Papel) is a Spanish heist crime drama television series created by Álex Pina. The show follows a mysterious man known as "The Professor" who recruits eight criminals to carry out a massive heist on the Royal Mint of Spain.

With its fast-paced storytelling, dynamic characters, and emotional rollercoaster of twists, Money Heist has captivated millions of fans around the world. The red jumpsuits and Salvador Dalí masks have become iconic symbols of resistance.
